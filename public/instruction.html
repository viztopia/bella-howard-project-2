Sichuan Mahjong Instructions 四川麻将游戏指导

Sichuan Mahjong is a popular variation of Mahjong known for its simple rules and fast-paced gameplay. Below is an introduction to the basic rules and common terms in Sichuan Mahjong to help beginners understand the game.四川麻将是一种广受欢迎的麻将变体，具有简单易懂的规则和快节奏的游戏风格。以下是四川麻将的一些基础玩法和常见术语，帮助新手更好地理解游戏。

Basic Rules

Sichuan Mahjong is usually played by four players using a set of 108 tiles (excluding wind and flower tiles). Each player aims to form specific combinations of tiles in their hand, and the first to complete a winning hand wins the round. 四川麻将通常由 四名玩家进行游戏，使用 108张牌（不含风牌和花牌）。每位玩家需要通过摸牌、出牌来组成特定的牌型组合，第一个组成指定牌型的玩家获胜。

Game Flow 游戏流程

  1. Each player starts with 13 tiles. 每位玩家有十三张初始牌
  2. The game begins with players drawing and discarding tiles in a clockwise order, discarding one unwanted tile after each draw.游戏开始时，按顺时针顺序轮流摸牌，并将其中一张不需要的牌打出。
  3. When a player completes a winning hand, they declare it and win the game. 当玩家凑齐胡牌所需的牌型时，可以宣告胡牌并获胜。

Common Terms 常见名词

Shunzi (Sequence) 顺子

Shunzi is a combination of three consecutive tiles in the same suit. For example: 2 Bamboo, 3 Bamboo, 4 Bamboo.顺子是由同一花色的连续三张牌组成的组合。例如：2万、3万、4万。

Duizi (Pair) 对子

Duizi is a pair of two identical tiles. A winning hand must contain at least one pair.对子是两张相同的牌。胡牌时，玩家必须至少有一个对子来组成完整的牌型

Kezi (Triplet) 刻子

Kezi is a combination of three identical tiles. For example: three 5 Circles.刻子是三张相同的牌的组合。例如：三张 5 条。

Hu Pai (Winning Hand) 胡牌

Hu Pai is the term for a winning hand, which consists of specific combinations of pairs, sequences, and triplets.胡牌是指玩家的手牌符合规定的牌型组合，即玩家的手中有一对对子和若干顺子或刻子，形成一个完整的牌型。

Common Winning Patterns 常见胡牌牌型

  • Peng Peng Hu 碰碰胡 - A hand made up entirely of triplets (Kezi).全部由刻子组成的胡牌。
  • Qing Yi Se 清一色 - A winning hand formed with tiles of only one suit.胡牌由同一花色组成
  • Qi Dui - A winning hand made up of seven pairs.由七个对子组成的胡牌。

Scoring 打分

In Sichuan Mahjong, the score after winning depends on the different tile combinations and bonus conditions met. Generally, rare patterns yield higher scores.
